If you're looking to create a website in Alicante, you're probably wondering: **how much will it cost?** The short answer is: it depends. But let's break it down so you know exactly what to expect.

## Price Ranges in Alicante (2026)

### Landing Page / Simple Corporate Website
**€800 - €2,500**

Ideal for small businesses, freelancers, or professionals who need a basic online presence.

**What's included:**
- 1-5 pages (home, services, contact, etc.)
- Responsive design (mobile + desktop)
- Contact form
- Basic SEO optimization
- Google Analytics integration

**Delivery time:** 2-3 weeks

### Complete Corporate Website
**€2,500 - €6,000**

For companies that need a robust professional presence with greater functionality.

**What's included:**
- 6-15 custom pages
- Blog/news system
- Advanced SEO optimization
- CRM integrations
- Content management system (CMS)
- Advanced forms
- Professional UX/UI design

**Delivery time:** 4-8 weeks

### Online Store (e-commerce)
**€3,500 - €12,000+**

For businesses selling products online.

**What's included:**
- Unlimited product catalog
- Shopping cart
- Payment gateway (Stripe, PayPal, Redsys)
- Stock management
- Order management panel
- Shipping calculator
- E-commerce SEO
- Conversion-focused design

**Delivery time:** 6-12 weeks

### Custom Web Platforms and Applications
**€10,000 - €50,000+**

For projects with specific functionalities: marketplaces, SaaS, customer portals, etc.

## Why Do Prices Vary So Much?

### 1. **Design Complexity**
A basic template costs less than a completely custom design from scratch.

### 2. **Features**
Each integration, booking system, customer area, or automation adds to the budget.

### 3. **Copywriting and Content**
Are you bringing written content or are we creating it? Professional copywriting has a cost.

### 4. **SEO and Marketing**
Search engine optimization requires keyword research, technical optimization, and content strategy.

### 5. **Maintenance and Support**
Some include free months of support, others charge monthly maintenance.

## What Should You NOT Accept for Less Than €800?

If someone offers you a "professional" website for €300-500, you're probably getting:
- An unmodified WordPress template
- No SEO optimization
- No original content
- Limited or no support
- Poor loading speed

**Result:** A website that exists but doesn't bring you clients.

## The Real Cost: Investment or Expense?

A website is not an expense, it's an **investment that should generate returns**.

Ask yourself:
- How many clients do I need to capture to recover the investment?
- What is the value of 24/7 visibility for my business?
- How much does NOT having a professional online presence cost me?
